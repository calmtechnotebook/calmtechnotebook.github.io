What we build on

Every app shares
the same principles.

01
Local-first
Your data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud dependency, no server to trust.
02
Zero tracking
No analytics, no crash reporting, no SDKs. Not even App Tracking Transparency — there's nothing to track.
03
Monochrome calm
Black and white interfaces. One accent color for the single most important action. No visual noise.
04
Pause built in
Every app includes a breathing exercise. A moment to step back, inhale, and use technology with intention.
